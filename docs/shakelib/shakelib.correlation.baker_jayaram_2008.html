shakelib.correlation.baker_jayaram_2008¶

shakelib.correlation.baker_jayaram_2008.baker_jayaram_correlation(t1, t2)[source]¶

Produce inter-period correlation for any two spectral periods.

Based upon: Baker, J.W. and Jayaram, N., “Correlation of spectral acceleration values from NGA ground motion models,” Earthquake Spectra, (2007).

Parameters:t2 (t1,) – The two periods of interest.
Returns:The predicted correlation coefficient
Return type:rho (float)
